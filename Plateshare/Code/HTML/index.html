Turning Extra Into Enough!

Our Vision

Simple really, beat global hunger and bring out the best of humanity using food as a medium.

The idea is to create self-sustained chapters across the world who will look after their local community. And in the process, inspire people around us to give back to those who need it most.

About Us

PlateShare is a platform that connects people with extra food to those who need it most. We help to reduce food waste and hunger by facilitating donations and connections within the community.

At PlateShare, we aim to bridge the gap between surplus food and hunger. Our mission is to prevent waste while ensuring no one goes hungry. Each day, households have excess food that often gets discarded, while millions face food insecurity. Through our volunteer-driven platform, we collect surplus food and redistribute it to those in need, including the homeless and struggling families. Our goal is not just to feed people but to foster empathy, reduce food waste, and build a sustainable, caring community. We believe surplus food can be an opportunity to support our neighbors and strengthen local bonds.
